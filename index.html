Homepage and Bio

Thanks for checking out my website! My name is Lilith (as you've probably figured out already) and I am an undergraduate at Indian Hills Community College in Ottumwa Iowa. Right now, I'm pursuing my associates degree in computer software development, and I'm always learning new things while I'm here. I plan to update this website as I progress through college to add to my list of skills, past projects, and fields of work I'm competent in.

I grew up in Centerville Iowa, where I have attended school all the way up to 12th grade at Centerville High School. I have been accepted to attend Indian Hills in person for the first time this fall, as all my other classes at Indian Hills have been through DualEnroll. I am registered with the National Honors Society, and am a registered Eagle Scout under my birth name with Scouting America.

In High School, I participated in a large variety of electives. I was in the Centerville Jazz I, Marching Band, & Ensemble Band as Lead Trombone, and Lead Bass in Choir. I took three years of a Spanish elective, I also took four years of science classes, along with some AP classes like College Composition and Statistics.

Outside of school, some personal hobbies of mine inlcude sketching, graphic design, painting, video game development/game theory, music, camping, and beekeeping.
